About the Author

I am Pavan Teja, a passionate writer and storyteller who dreams of becoming a filmmaker. I love creating stories and novels that come from deep emotions like love, pain, and human struggles. For me, storytelling is not just about writing—it is about bringing my imagination to life. That is why I take my stories a step further by creating AI-generated images and AI video musical trailers, turning them into a visual blueprint or storyboard. Through this, I prepare my stories for the day I get an opportunity to transform them into films and share them with the world.
